# Potential Features — Artificial Heart

Brainstormed, thematically appropriate feature ideas. Nothing here is committed or implemented; these are
starting points for future planning.

The mod's identity: **"all the atmosphere, none of the side effects"** — tamed, decorative versions of
blocks whose vanilla behavior gets in the way of building. Every idea below follows that pitch.

## More pale garden decor

* **Dormant Creaking Heart** — a third visual state (the dim/dormant look) to complement the active-face
  variant, so builders can pick exactly which stage of the heart they want.
* **Heartbeat toggle** — a purely cosmetic option (wrench or shears interaction) that makes the Detached
  Creaking Heart softly pulse its glow, without any of the gameplay behavior.
* **Clipped Pale Hanging Moss** — pale moss strands frozen at a chosen length; never grow, never sway
  into a shape you didn't build.
* **Artificial Resin Veins** — placeable resin "seams" for pale oak builds that never came from a real
  creaking heart.
* **Inert Creaking Statue** — a creaking-shaped decorative block/entity (like an armor stand) that stands
  where you pose it. The ultimate garden gnome for a pale garden build.

## Tamed versions of other "noisy" blocks

* **Muted Sculk Shrieker** — all the sculk texture, zero warden risk, no shriek.
* **Still Sculk Sensor** — frozen mid-pulse or dormant; never triggers, never wobbles.
* **Silent Spore Blossom** — a spore blossom with the particle drip disabled (or a config for particle
  density), for players who love the flower but not the FPS confetti.
* **Empty Bee Nest** — the nest texture as pure decoration; no bees move in, nothing to anger.
* **Unlit / Ever-lit Candle variants?** — candles frozen in a lit look without being a light source, or
  vice versa (stretch idea; may be out of scope).

## Conversion workflow

* **Consistent "taming" verbs** — extend the shears/axe pattern: shears clip plants, an axe detaches
  wood-adjacent blocks, and perhaps a **brush** "stills" sculk. One tool family per block family keeps
  it intuitive.
* **Un-taming** — right-click a decorative variant with a *different* tool to convert it back to the
  vanilla block, so no build decision is permanent.
* **Waxing metaphor** — alternatively (or additionally), use honeycomb to "freeze" a block in its current
  state, mirroring copper waxing. Vanilla players already know this verb.

## Redstone & light hooks (optional, config-gated)

* **Light-level dial on the Detached Creaking Heart** — wrench-cycle the emitted light from 0–15.
* **Comparator support** — the detached heart outputs a fixed, configurable comparator signal, making it
  a stylish redstone constant for pale-garden-themed contraptions.

## Polish

* **Potted clipped eyeblossoms in more pots** — support for decorated-pot planting if vanilla ever allows.
* **Advancement: "Change of Heart"** — detach your first creaking heart.
* **Advancement: "Garden Variety"** — collect every decorative variant the mod adds.
