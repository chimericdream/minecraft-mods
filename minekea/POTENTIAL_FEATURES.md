# Potential Features — Minekea

Brainstormed, thematically appropriate feature ideas. Nothing here is committed or implemented; these are
starting points for future planning.

The mod's identity: **functional, decorative furniture "some assembly required"** — storage that displays,
furniture you can use, and finishing touches for every room. Ideas are grouped by room, IKEA-showroom style.

## Finish what's started

* **Iron shelves** — already on the README's planned list; extend to other metals (copper shelves that
  oxidize, gold for the treasure room).
* **Wallpaper** — a wallpaper texture is already credited in the README but not listed as a feature;
  dye-able wallpaper panels (with matching trim) would complete the walls the covers started.
* **Other hidden/disguised storage** — the planned bullet, made concrete below (see "Secrets & storage").

## Living room & study

* **Couches and benches** — multi-seat furniture using the seat entity, with corner pieces that connect
  like beams do (wrench-toggleable connections).
* **Armchairs and cushioned variants** — existing chairs plus wool/dye cushion overlays.
* **Coffee and end tables** — lower-profile tables that visually connect with existing table types.
* **Grandfather clock** — a 2-block-tall clock whose face tracks in-game time; ticks softly (toggleable).
* **Fireplace mantel** — a decorative frame block designed to sit around a campfire safely (fireproof
  trim), because every showroom living region needs a hearth.
* **Rolling library ladder** — attaches to bookshelf walls and slides along them; pairs perfectly with
  the existing variant bookshelves.

## Kitchen & dining

* **Counters and cabinets** — kitchen-height storage in all wood types, with drawer and door fronts;
  cabinet interiors work like small chests.
* **Sink** — decorative water source block (infinite water toggle in config); dispenser-fillable.
* **Bar stools** — taller foot stools for counter seating.
* **Plate and mug items** — placeable tableware for set dining tables; jars already store food, plates
  display it.

## Bedroom & textiles

* **Curtains** — dye-able, two-tall, open/close on right-click (or redstone), matching bed colors.
* **Wardrobes** — 2-block-tall storage that displays a stored armor set on its doors (armor-stand-style
  mannequin behind glass as an upgrade).
* **Rugs** — patterned carpets (weave patterns per wood-adjacent fiber types, or banner-pattern-driven).
* **Bunk beds / loft frames** — decorative bed framing for compact builder housing.

## Walls, windows & trim

* **Crown molding and baseboards** — trim blocks in the beam/cover family for finishing interior walls.
* **Interior window frames** — complements the existing shutters (which handle the exterior).
* **Room divider screens** — folding panel blocks in wood + paper (shoji-style) variants.
* **Mirrors** — polished block with a subtle fake-reflection shimmer (no real reflections needed; even a
  brightened parallax texture reads as "mirror").

## Garden & exterior

* **Planter boxes and hanging planters** — placeable soil containers accepting any vanilla plant;
  window-box variant snaps under windows.
* **Trellis** — climbable lattice that vines/glow berries can grow along, in all wood types.
* **Weathervane** — spins decoratively; points a consistent random direction per placement.
* **Mailbox** — small per-player-openable container at the property line; flag raises when a new item is
  inside (multiplayer flavor).
* **Garden arch** — beam-family curved arch pieces for paths and entrances.

## Secrets & storage (expanding the disguised-storage promise)

* **False floor hatch** — a trapdoor that perfectly mimics the covering block (cover-family camouflage).
* **Hollow books** — a bookshelf-slot item with a small hidden inventory; the bookshelf looks identical.
* **Painting safe** — item-frame-style painting that swings open over a 1-block cavity.
* **Disguised crates** — crates skinned as ordinary building blocks, wrench-revealed.

## Tools & systems polish

* **Furniture Catalog** — a Patchouli "showroom catalog" auto-listing every Minekea block with its
  recipe: the in-game IKEA catalog the mod name promises.
* **Block painter extensions** — let the painter recolor the new textiles (curtains, rugs, cushions).
* **Wrench connection modes for new families** — couches, counters, and trim reuse the beam
  connect/disconnect interaction so the "assembly" language stays consistent mod-wide.
